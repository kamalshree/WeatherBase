Wherever you're located, you can use the WeatherBase app for Android (Android version 4.1+ and above) to locate, Bookmark, and even find more about weather Forecast.

Tip: Make sure you are have mobile data on for more smooth functioning of the WeatherBase app.

  • You can select any location on the map and add it to your Favourite Sections.
  • By Navigating to your Home Screen you can view all the favourite places displayed.
